Gulab Jamun :


Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Maida
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make the sphere of maida
  2. Prepare sugar water for jamun
  3. Fry sphere in oil
  4. Put it in Sugar water and wait for 15 min

Kaju katli :


Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • cashew powder mixture
  • Kaju dough

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews to a fine powder
  2. Prepare the sugar & cashew powder mixture
  3. Knead the Kaju dough & apply the silver varq
  4. Cut into diamonds and indulge!
